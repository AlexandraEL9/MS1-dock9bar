Our Ethos

Nestled in the vibrant Salford Quays, our craft brewery and bar embodies the essence of community and innovation. With a commitment to quality and creativity, we blend traditional brewing techniques with modern flair, producing a diverse range of flavorful brews that reflect the local spirit. Our cozy yet dynamic space fosters a welcoming atmosphere where beer enthusiasts and newcomers alike gather to share stories, savor exceptional brews, and celebrate the artistry of craft beer. Embracing the industrial heritage of the area, we pay homage to Salford Quays' rich history while embracing a forward-thinking approach to brewing.
